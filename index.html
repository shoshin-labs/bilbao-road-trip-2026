Travel first

The route, written as a driving timeline

The trip has a simple spine: one overnight on the way down, Bilbao in the middle, and a tidy Bordeaux finish before the return ferry. The biggest value here is in the timing, so the cards lean into departure windows and arrival targets.

Day 1 · Fri 4 Sep Jersey → La Rochelle

Ferry out, then drive to the first overnight

Suggested leave Jersey08:45
Ferry departure09:40
Road time~4.5–5.5 hrs after disembarkation
Target check-in18:00

Plan: get across the Channel in the morning, keep the drive moving, and arrive in La Rochelle with enough daylight left for a harbour walk and a very easy first dinner.

Day 2 · Sat 5 Sep La Rochelle → Bilbao

The biggest driving day of the trip

Suggested depart07:30
Lunch stop12:30–13:30
Target arrival17:30–18:30

Plan: leave early, treat the drive like a transfer rather than a sightseeing day, and arrive at the Bilbao base in time to unpack before dinner.

Day 9 · Sat 12 Sep Bilbao → Bordeaux

Shorter drive, cleaner finish

Suggested depart08:30
Target arrival13:30–14:30
Hotel check-in15:00

Plan: roll out of Bilbao after breakfast, park once in Bordeaux, and leave the rest of the evening for a slow walk and a final dinner on foot.

Day 10 · Sun 13 Sep Bordeaux → Jersey

Return day, with a big ferry buffer

Suggested depart09:00
St Malo bufferArrive well before 18:00
Ferry home18:00

Plan: avoid a rushed morning, keep a generous buffer for the ferry leg, and end the loop back in Jersey without trying to squeeze in too much on the last day.
