Travel first

The trip as a timeline

Start with the movement, not the hotels. The first two days are the long southbound push; the middle is Bilbao; the last two days tidy the loop back to Jersey.

  1. 1
    Fri 4 Sep Jersey → La Rochelle

    Ferry out, drive south, first night in La Rochelle

    Morning: leave Jersey on the 09:40 ferry to St Malo. Midday: drive across France. Evening: check into La Rochelle, walk the harbour, keep dinner easy, and make it an early night.

  2. 2
    Sat 5 Sep La Rochelle → Bilbao

    The biggest driving day of the trip

    Early start: leave La Rochelle after breakfast. Late afternoon: arrive at the Bilbao base, unpack, and protect the first evening from trying to do too much.

  3. 3
    Sun 6 Sep Bilbao

    First proper Bilbao day

    Morning: Casco Viejo and the river. Afternoon: lunch, a reset, and a slower pace. Evening: a flexible pintxos wander rather than a fixed plan.

  4. 4
    Mon 7 Sep Bilbao

    Museum day and city wandering

    Morning: Guggenheim Bilbao. Afternoon: the riverfront and the Ensanche. Night: book one good meal if the mood suits it.

  5. 5
    Tue 8 Sep Bilbao + coast

    Getxo or Portugalete if the weather plays ball

    Late morning: head to the waterfront. Afternoon: stay light, stay outside, and come back to Bilbao for a calm evening.

  6. 6
    Wed 9 Sep Bilbao

    Optional inland day or a proper rest day

    Option A: Rioja or another inland run. Option B: keep the car parked and use the extra time for slow lunches, galleries, and recovery.

  7. 7
    Thu 10 Sep Bilbao

    Second excursion or slower city day

    Option A: San Sebastián or another coast run. Option B: stay in Bilbao and let the week breathe with cafés, walks, and a longer lunch.

  8. 8
    Fri 11 Sep Bilbao

    Final Bilbao full day

    Morning: packing and coffee. Afternoon: one last wander. Evening: the final Bilbao dinner before the road south again.

  9. 9
    Sat 12 Sep Bilbao → Bordeaux

    Shorter drive, final base

    Morning: leave Bilbao. Afternoon: arrive in Bordeaux, check in, park once, then leave the car alone and finish the day on foot.

  10. 10
    Sun 13 Sep Bordeaux → Jersey

    Return to Jersey

    Morning: leave Bordeaux in good time. Evening: take the 18:00 ferry from St Malo and close the loop back home.

How to read the trip

Move hard at the edges, slow down in the middle.

The route is built to absorb the long drives early and late. Bilbao gets the breathing space; the ferry days are the ones to respect.

Trip rhythm

The pacing underneath the article

It is intentionally front-loaded with driving, then calmer through the middle, then neat again at the end.

Move early

The first ferry and first drive are the ones that set the tone. Get them done, then stop trying to make every day a big day.

Stay put in Bilbao

Bilbao is the centre of gravity. Treat the apartment like the base camp and let the city and day trips orbit around it.

Finish clean

Bordeaux is the tidy last stop: enough time to settle, park, and then leave the ferry day free of extra moving parts.
